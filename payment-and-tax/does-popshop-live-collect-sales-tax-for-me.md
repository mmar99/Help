# Does Popshop Live collect sales tax for me?

During the initial setup of your seller profile, you must input the sales tax for your region (to do so, please tap the settings icon). We collect sales tax from your customer at checkout on your behalf and remit it to you. All payouts include tax, and you can request a tax form at the end of each year.
